pgFocus autofocus module

I've tested Karl Bellve's pgFocus open source and open hardware autofocus module. This module maintains the focus of a microscope overtime by sending a control signal that adjusts the position of the microscope objective. The control signal is generated in response to the displacement of an infrared beam hitting a linear light sensor array. The beam is reflected off of the coverslip/water interface.
